About Our Abuja-Based Delicacies

From the heart of Nigeria's capital, Oma's Frosted Bites crafts premium snacks using the finest locally-sourced ingredients to satisfy your cravings while nourishing your body.

Proudly Made in Abuja

Our kitchen in Abuja's vibrant culinary scene allows us to blend traditional Nigerian flavors with modern healthy snack innovations, creating unique treats you won't find elsewhere.

We use real tropical fruits, indigenous nuts, and wholesome grains sourced from Nigerian farmers. All products are free from artificial preservatives and additives - just pure, honest nutrition.
